Caring Beyond Business

17% of our valued customers are cancer patients, and we are proud to support them with special discounts to make their journey a little easier.

We also collaborate with distributors who cater to cancer patients, offering discounts to ensure affordability for those in need.

Bulk Order Process

Understand our step-by-step process for bulk orders.

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out to us via WhatsApp or email for your bulk order inquiries.

Step 2: Free Sampling

We prepare and ship a free sample to you via DHL for quality assurance.

Step 3: Approval

Once you approve the sample, we finalize the order details with you.

Step 4: Manufacturing

We either deliver from stock or begin manufacturing custom orders.

Step 5: Delivery

Your bulk order is delivered on time via trusted logistics partners.
